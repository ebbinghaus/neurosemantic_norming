Rating words and features

Other times you may be judging the relationship of features you have already seen to words you haven't seen yet, like now judging whether "birth" is associated with interactions between people and whether it is a natural thing.

You should simply make the judgments on each screen without concern for previous responses you have given either to that word or to other words with shared features.

So if you rated banana highly on is a natural thing it is OK to also rate birth highly on natural, and rating birth highly on relationships doesn't mean you can't also rate it high (or low) on the is a natural thing scale.
